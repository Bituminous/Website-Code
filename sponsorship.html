Sponsorship

Since Mythic Mechanix is a volunteer-run team, we solely depend on donations from supporters like you. All of the donations will be used to improve our supply including tools and materials necessary for the robot as well as registration fees to compete in upcoming events. If you would like to sponsor our team, please support us at our gofundme page.

$1500+ - Legendary Donor:

  • Have your name/logo featured on the robot
  • All previous tier awards

$1000-1499 - V.I.P. Donor:

  • Have your name/logo featured in the pit during competition
  • 2nd free Mythic Mechanix Team t-shirt
  • Special thank-you post on social media
  • All previous tier awards

$500-999 - Premium Donor:

  • Have your name/logo featured on t-shirt
  • All previous tier awards

$100-499 - Essential Part:

  • Have your name/logo featured on our website

  • All previous tier awards
